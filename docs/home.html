About Us

UniFix is an advanced platform designed to help schools and colleges efficiently monitor and manage uniform compliance. By leveraging AI-driven technology, we automate the detection and reporting of uniform violations, saving valuable time and resources.

Our mission is to support educational institutions in maintaining discipline while reducing the manual workload of staff. With features like real-time notifications, analytics, and a user-friendly interface, UniFix ensures smooth implementation at all levels of education.

We are committed to fostering a positive learning environment by simplifying uniform compliance checks and enabling educators to focus on what truly matters: teaching and learning.
